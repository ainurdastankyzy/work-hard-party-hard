About Us

The Summer Work and Travel Program gives international students the opportunity to live and work in the United States during their summer vacations, as well as to live a normal life among ordinary Americans. Program participants work in the hospitality industry, in ancillary restaurants, lifeguards or amusement park assistants. Participants in the program must be undergraduate students outside the United States and have completed at least one semester of their course. In addition, they should be fluent in English. Kazakh university students can work in the United States from May 25 to September 1 and must return to Kazakhstan by the start of the fall semester. The Summer Work & Travel program is not for tourists who only travel to see the United States. For tourists in the United States, another visa is provided - tourist B-2.
